About LostMate

LostMate is a simple and effective platform that helps people report and search for lost or found items. Whether you've misplaced something or found a valuable belonging, our system connects the right people to return it safely. We aim to build a community of trust, honesty, and care by making lost and found reporting easy and accessible.
